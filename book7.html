"The Hobbit" by J.R.R. Tolkien:

Background: J.R.R. Tolkien, an English writer, philologist, and academic, created this fantasy novel. "The Hobbit" is a prelude to his epic trilogy "The Lord of the Rings" and follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit, as he embarks on an adventure filled with dragons, dwarves, and magic.
