"Ikigai" by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles

The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life" is a book written by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles.Published in 2016, the book explores the concept of "ikigai," a Japanese term that combines "iki" (life) and "gai" (value or worth). It delves into the principles of finding purpose and joy in one's life by discovering the intersection of passion, vocation, profession, and mission.
